General

AfriCAD is a premier online platform delivering professional CAD/CAM training across Africa. It offers accessible courses like AutoCAD Fundamentals and Fusion 360 Complete, featuring video lessons, hands-on projects, and expert feedback. Students enroll, access materials 24/7, submit assignments, and earn certificates to advance their careers.

Visit the AfriCAD website, select a course (e.g., AutoCAD Fundamentals), and sign up for an account. After enrolling, you’ll gain immediate access to course content, including videos and assignments, allowing you to learn at your own pace with support from instructors and the community.

You’ll need a PC or Mac with a stable internet connection, a modern browser (e.g., Chrome, Edge), and CAD software like AutoCAD or Fusion 360. Recommended specs include 8GB RAM, a multi-core processor, and 10GB free storage. Mobile devices can access course materials but are not ideal for software tasks.
